What is Skillsme Test Committee

The Skillsme Committee is a non-profit organization funded by Skillsme. It is focusing on provide technical suggestions and guidance for the improvement of the tests of Skillsme in various programming languages, and give new ideas to help the creation of test questions .

An Open Organization

Skillsme Test Committee is an open non-profit organisation running beside of Skillsme. it is devoting to bring skilled developers together, share and contribute their wisdoms to revolutionise the way we identify talents, and bring the latest directions in IT industry into Skillsme. The committee will be formed by Skillsme but running by their own board and leadership to hold its fairness and opnness.

Our Mission

Keep Skillsme updated with the latest market needs and hold high quality standard for our question.

Our Vision

Let programmers be tested and identified by people who understand programming.

We love ideas

Give different points of view and opinions to keep the test creative and real.

Function of committee

Provide Skillsme the directions of question, as explore the trend of IT industry, give direction for Skillsme test strategy and help the tests keep up with the market. Also help Skillsme to refine the test structures for each coding language, decide the categories of tests to fit in real work scenario, and hep us to find problems as to Review the questions before they are applied into real tests, and give advice and suggestions to help us assure the overall quality of our tests.

A Global Organization

We welcome all developers from all over the world to join us. With different views from different market, we can get the big picture of the global IT industry. With the top developers around the world, we can make our tests become an important guideline for talent employment in different regions, and give a fair chance to every developers everywhere.

We are family

This is not just a committee. This is also a friendly group that can help developers in many ways.

  • Gain access to any event raised by Skillsme for free
  • Meet the greatest developers and make new friends
  • Get the latest updates of the IT industry
  • Provide help to other members
